Frequently Asked Questions

What are the clinic operating hours?

The clinic is open daily from 04:00 PM to 10:00 PM for OPD and scheduled visits. Emergency services are prioritized.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

While walk-in patients are always welcome, we highly recommend booking an appointment online through this website to minimize your waiting time.

Are all laboratory tests conducted in-house?

Yes, we have a fully equipped laboratory that handles the majority of routine and specialized medical tests internally to ensure quick results.

Is parking available at the clinic?

Yes, there is designated and secure parking available for all visiting patients at Saleem Medical Complex.
